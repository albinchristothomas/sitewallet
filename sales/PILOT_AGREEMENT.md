**Between:** RigWise (operated by [Your legal name / company], "RigWise")
**And:** [Pilot company name] ("Pilot Partner")
**Date:** [date]
**Pilot site(s):** [well / LSD or "one gate, Pilot Partner's choice"]

### 1. What this is
A **free, 30-day trial** of the RigWise gate-verification app at the pilot
site(s). No fee. Either side can end it any time, for any reason, with a
message — no notice period, no penalty.

### 2. What RigWise provides
- Access to the app for the Pilot Partner's medic(s) and workers.
- Setup help and a person to call when something breaks.
- The end-of-day reports and roster generated during the pilot.

### 3. What the Pilot Partner does
- Uses the app for real gate sign-ins during the pilot.
- Tells RigWise what works and what doesn't (this is the whole point).
- Doesn't share login access outside its own medics/workers.

### 4. Data — who owns what
- **Workers own their credentials.** A worker's wallet stays with the worker.
- **The Pilot Partner owns its operational records** (its rosters, its EOD
  reports). RigWise will export them on request and delete them on request.
- **RigWise owns the software.** Nothing here transfers the app or its code.
- RigWise will **not sell** Pilot Partner or worker data, ever.

### 5. Privacy & security
- Data is stored on managed cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and
  at rest. RigWise is targeting Canadian data residency (AWS ca-central-1) and
  PIPEDA alignment for production. See the Privacy One-Pager.
- Personal data is retained only as long as needed for site safety records
  (target: 2 years after last activity) then deleted.

### 6. Who decides at the gate
RigWise is a **tool**. The medic always makes the final admit/deny call; any
override is the medic's decision and is logged. RigWise is not responsible for
admission decisions or for incidents on site.

### 7. No warranty / limited liability (pilot)
The pilot software is provided "as is." During the free pilot, RigWise's total
liability is limited to $0 (it's free). Neither side is liable to the other for
indirect or consequential losses.

### 8. Confidentiality
Each side keeps the other's non-public information (pricing, worker data,
business details) confidential and uses it only for the pilot.

### 9. After the pilot
No obligation to continue. If both want to keep going, a paid agreement replaces
this one. If not, RigWise exports/deletes the Pilot Partner's data on request.
